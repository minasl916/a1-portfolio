About Me

I'm a 4th year UC Irvine student majoring in Computer Science and Engineering.

During my time in college, I discovered a strong passion in Social Media Marketing, where I could combine my creativity and strategic skills. Through hands-on experience, specifically through serving as Online Media Director for my sorority, Kappa Alpha Theta, I've been able to further develop skills in content creation, digital branding and audience engagement.

Originally from New York City, I grew up in a fast-paced, diverse environment that shaped my drive, adaptability, and appreciation for new and bold ideas. Additionally, moving to Southern California for college, the shift in environment introduced me to a more creative, and design-forward culture. Many ask me which I prefer - New York or California? I truly feel both cities represent who I am: ambitious, creative, and dedicated to building meaningful experiences.
